Study title: Nexus User Testing

Researcher details: Dr Scott Hale, Senior Data Scientist, Oxford Internet Institute

Purpose of study: We will ask you questions about a network diagram in order to understand how easy different visualizations are to comprehend.

In this task you will be given a quick introduction to reading network diagrams and then asked 30 questions about a network diagram showing migrant stock. The introduction will prepare you to answer these questions correctly, and we will pay you a bonus of £0.05 for each correct answer in addition to the base payment of £1.00. This means you could recieve up to £2.50 for participating. Finally, we will ask a small number of questions about your background. The task should take approximately 12 to 15 minutes.
